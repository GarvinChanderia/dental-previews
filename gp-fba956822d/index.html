Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

For most people, twice a year for routine cleanings and checkups is ideal.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional whitening supervised by a dentist is safe and gives even results.

Are dental implants permanent?

With proper care, implants can last decades — often a lifetime.

Does a root canal hurt?

With modern anaesthesia, root canals are no more uncomfortable than a routine filling.

When should my child's first visit be?

By their first birthday or within 6 months of the first tooth appearing.
